Have a question?
Here are some answers you might be looking for!

Where to find more details on a product?

If the pictures and descriptions are not enough, message me on instagram @_noursabbaghh for more details.

How are shipping costs evaluated?

Unfortunately, it is not possible to evaluate shipping costs until the order has been delivered, it will then be either cash on delivery or the courrier will arrive with a visa machine if you would like to pay by card.

Where do you ship to?

Currently, delivery options are only available to the cities that are in West Yorkshire. This will definitely change at some point in the future.

What happens if I dont like a product?

Since the clothes are already used it does not make sense to return them and then ship them to other customers further. Also there is no current solution to returning the products back to us. There is open room for discussion on returning items, you can message me on instagram @_noursabbaghh.

How often do you upload new products?

Around every two weeks or so. If you dont find any products left, it means that everything is sold out and that I will be uploading soon.

If I have any other questions, where do I contact you?

Feel free to message me on instagram @_noursabbaghh at any time for any extra questions!
